Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water or hot coffee

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, then mix until smooth.
  4. Slowly add hot water (or coffee) and mix (the batter will be thin).
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool before slicing or adding frosting.
