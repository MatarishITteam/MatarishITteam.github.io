Steak & Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2 steak cuts (ribeye or sirloin)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4–5 potatoes (peeled & chopped)
  • 3 tbsp butter (for mashed potatoes)
  • ½ cup milk or cream
  • Salt & pepper

Directions:

  1. Boil the potatoes in salted water for 15–20 minutes until soft, then drain
  2. Mash the potatoes with butter, milk (or cream), salt, and pepper until smooth and creamy. Set aside.
  3. Season the steaks well with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over high heat
  5. Sear the steaks for 3–4 minutes perside (depending on thickness and desired doneness).
  6. Add butter, garlic, and herbs to thepan, then spoon the melted butter over the steaks for extra flavor.
  7. Remove steaks from heat and let them rest for 5 minutes.
  8. Serve the steak hot with a side of creamy mashed potatoes
