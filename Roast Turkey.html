Thanksgiving Roast Turkey Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 whole turkey (4–6 kg)
  • 100g butter (softened)
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 onion (quartered)
  • 1 lemon (halved)
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, sage)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 2–3 cups chicken broth

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Pat the turkey dry, then rub it all over with butter, olive oil, salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
  3. Stuff the cavity with onion, lemon, and fresh herbs.
  4. Place the turkey in a roasting pan and pour chicken broth into the bottom.
  5. Roast for about 3–4 hours (depending on size), basting every 30–40 minutes with the pan juices.
  6. Check that the internal temperature is fully cooked, then remove from oven.
  7. Let it rest for 20–30 minutes before carving and serving.
