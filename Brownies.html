Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (225g) butter (melted)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C and line a baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix melted butter, sugar,and cocoa powder until combined.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each, then add vanilla.
  4. Stir in flour, salt, and baking powder until just combined (don’t overmix).
  5. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  6. Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for 20 25 minutes (center should be slightly soft for fudgy brownies).
  8. Let cool completely before cutting into squares.
