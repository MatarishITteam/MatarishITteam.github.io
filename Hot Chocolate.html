Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ cup dark or milk chocolate(chopped)
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows(optional)

Directions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat (don’t let it boil).
  2. Add cocoa powder, sugar, and salt,then whisk until smooth.
  3. Add the chopped chocolate and stir until fully melted.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream or marshmallows if desired
